### **Executive Summary** 

Nelo is building the world’s first **UGC \+ Influencer marketplace infrastructure** designed around **brand cultural identity, community trust, and creator-led offline activation**, not just content production or reach metrics.

While existing platforms optimise for volume, virality, and short-term performance, Nelo treats **trust as infrastructure** and **culture as a performance lever**. The platform connects brands to **brand-literate creators** who understand brand language, audience culture, and community dynamics, enabling credible influence both online and in real-world community activations, without Nelo operating events itself.

V1 launches as a web-based marketplace with:

* AI-powered creator–brand matching built on cultural alignment and trust fit,

* creator-led community activation as a new service layer in the creator economy,

* trust and performance forecasting, dynamic pricing, and compliance intelligence,

* and enterprise-ready integrations across eCommerce, CRM, affiliate, social, email, and analytics stacks (UK, US & Global).

##  **AI Model: Community Activation Matching Engine (CAME)**

### **Feature**

Community Activation Matching Engine (CAME)

---

### **Description**

An AI-powered matching and orchestration engine that connects **brands** with **UGC creators and micro-influencers** capable of executing **offline community activations** (street activations, campus activations, local pop-ups, community demos, sampling, meetups) on behalf of brands.

Instead of Nelo managing physical events, the AI identifies:

* Which creators are best positioned to activate specific communities

* Which brands are best matched to those creators’ local communities

* How activations should be structured and compensated

* What outcomes to expect from each activation

This allows Nelo to scale **community activation as a distributed creator service**, not as a centralised events company.

No existing UGC or influencer platform treats **offline community activation** as a **creator service marketplace**.

Nelo’s model:

* Turns community activation into a **creator-powered service layer**

* Creates a new creator economy category: **“Community Activation Creators”**
