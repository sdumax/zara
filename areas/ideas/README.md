# Ideas & Projects

**Status:** Always flowing — capture everything, evaluate ruthlessly

---

## How This Works

1. **Capture** — new idea hits? Drop it here fast, no filter. Use `../../templates/project-brief.md` for anything with legs.
2. **Evaluate** — once a week, review the backlog. Score each idea (Impact / Effort / Excitement).
3. **Promote** — if an idea earns it, it gets its own folder in `areas/` or gets added to the startup roadmap.
4. **Archive** — ideas that don't make the cut stay here for reference. Nothing gets deleted.

---

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Impact | Effort | Excitement |
|-------|--------|--------|------------|
| 1 | Minimal | Huge | Meh |
| 2 | Moderate | Medium | Interested |
| 3 | High | Low | Fired up |

**Sweet spot:** High impact + Low effort + Fired up = do it now.
